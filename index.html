Chocolate Pizza

Posted 15 december 2013 / dessert

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over mediun heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the fig and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chuncky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or tow stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: in a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolve and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set the mixture in the frisge and wait until the mixture is cooled.

Using an icecream amchine, pour liquids into the frozen and process according to manufacturue instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/-10 minutes.Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

◻1 1/2 cups milk

◻1 1/2 cups mascarpone

◻1/2 tsp pink salt

◻1 lb Black Mission Figs

◻1/2 cup brown sugar

◻2-4 tbsp water

◻1 1/2 cups heavy cream

◻1/3 granulated sugar

◻2 egg yolks

◻1 lemon, juiced

◻2 tbsp butter

◻1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
